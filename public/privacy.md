# Privacy Policy - PermitFlow Pro

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

PermitFlow Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the **Oman Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)**.

> [!NOTE]
> Official Arabic version available upon request as per Omani regulatory requirements.

## 1. Data Collection & AI Processing
We collect and process images of HVAC nameplates to provide automated compliance audits.
- **Gemini AI Disclosure**: We use Google Gemini AI models to process and analyze images for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and compliance verification. By using our service, you consent to this AI-driven processing.

## 2. Data Storage & Security
- **Supabase**: Your data (audit logs, user profiles) is stored securely in **Supabase**.
- **Region**: Content is hosted in the **Middle East (Me-central1)** region to ensure low latency and compliance with regional data residency expectations where applicable.

## 3. Payments & Merchant of Record
- **Paddle**: All financial transactions and subscriptions are handled by **Paddle.com**.
- **Merchant of Record**: Paddle is the Merchant of Record for all our orders. They provide all customer service and handle returns.

## 4. Oman Data Subject Rights
Under the Oman PDPL, you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access**: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectify**: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erase**: You may request the deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at compliance@permitflow.pro.

## 5. Contact Us
For any privacy-related inquiries in the Sultanate of Oman or internationally, please reach out to our Data Protection Officer at dpo@permitflow.pro.
